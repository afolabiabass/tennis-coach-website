Hello! I’m Ibrahim Babatunde Balogun, a Tennis Coach, sports lover and enthusiast.

I found my passion for Tennis while working as a ballboy at the well-renowned Ikoyi Club in Lagos state in 2003. By 2006 I had earned the award of Ball Boy Champion. In 2012, I Started working as a tennis coach in Greensprings school in Laogos State after years of training as an athelete and coach. In 2017, I moved on to the Lagos preparatory school in ikoyi, Lagos state. I joined the Luik Tennis and Recreation center as the Head coach, training any level of age from 3years - 70years. My duties also included training the club members and designing tennis programs that were tailored to my different types of clients and their work schedules, while supervising and monitoring their growth and improvements.
